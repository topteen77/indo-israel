End-to-end International Recruitment Services

We manage the entire recruitment lifecycle — from sourcing skilled candidates to compliant cross-border deployment — so employers can scale their workforce confidently and candidates can move safely for better opportunities.

Our Services

Workforce Sourcing & Screening

We source skilled candidates from trusted talent pools and screen them using competency-based interviews, background checks and employer-fit evaluation to reduce hiring risk.

  • Targeted talent search
  • Pre-employment screening & background checks
  • Video interviews & reference validation

Trade Skill Assessments

Practical and certified assessments for trade and technical roles to ensure candidates meet job-specific performance standards before placement.

  • Hands-on practical tests
  • Skill verification reports
  • Training gap analysis

Employer Workforce Deployment

From offer management to onboarding, we coordinate employer engagement, placement timelines and post-deployment support to ensure smooth workforce absorption.

  • Offer & contract facilitation
  • Onboarding coordination
  • Post-assignment follow-up

Documentation & Visa Coordination

End-to-end documentation, medical checks and visa processing with local partners to reduce delays and ensure legal entry and employment.

  • Document verification & attestation
  • Visa application support
  • Pre-departure orientation

Regulatory Compliance Management

We maintain a regulation-first approach across source and destination countries to protect workers and shield employers from legal risk.

  • Labour law and licensing advisory
  • Employment contract compliance
  • Audit-ready record keeping

Cross-border Workforce Logistics

Comprehensive logistics including travel, arrival support and local transfers so candidates arrive safe, ready and on-time.

  • Travel and arrival coordination
  • Accommodation & local transport setup
  • 24/7 helpline during relocation
