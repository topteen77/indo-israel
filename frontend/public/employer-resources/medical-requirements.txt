MEDICAL REQUIREMENTS — REFERENCE NOTES
======================================
Apravas · Employer resources — Not medical advice. Follow official PIBA / clinic guidance.

- Workers may be required to complete medical examination at authorised facilities before deployment.
- Typical areas assessed: general fitness, communicable disease screening, vision/hearing where role-relevant.
- Certificates often must be recent (validity window set by authority or employer policy).
- Keep copies for compliance files; respect privacy and data protection rules.
- For Israel-bound placements, confirm current PIBA-approved medical centre list from official sources.

Always verify current rules with official government portals and your legal advisor.
