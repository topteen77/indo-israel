PRE-DEPARTURE CHECKLIST (WORKER)
================================
Apravas · Employer resources — Adapt to your programme and destination requirements.

□ Valid passport (minimum validity per embassy / airline rules)
□ Work permit / visa approval documentation copies
□ Employment contract signed (where required before travel)
□ Medical fitness certificate (PIBA / employer-approved facility if applicable)
□ Insurance: travel / health as required
□ Tickets and arrival logistics confirmed
□ Emergency contacts shared (employer + worker)
□ Orientation pack: site address, reporting manager, first-day schedule
□ Local accommodation / transport handover (if provided)
□ Banking / payroll setup instructions communicated
