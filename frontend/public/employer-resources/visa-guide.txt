VISA & WORK PERMIT — ORIENTATION GUIDE (SUMMARY)
=================================================
Apravas · Employer resources — High-level steps only; laws change. Use official portals.

Typical flow (Israel employment from India — illustrative):
1. Employer sponsorship / work permit application through PIBA channels where applicable
2. Worker documentation: passport, contracts, medical, police clearance, education/experience proofs as required
3. Consulate / visa application steps per current embassy instructions
4. Pre-departure briefing: rights, reporting lines, emergency procedures
5. Post-arrival registration or reporting if required by host country

Official references (verify URLs periodically):
- India eMigrate programme (where applicable to your corridor)
- Israel Population and Immigration Authority (PIBA)
- Relevant embassy / consulate sites

This guide does not replace legal or immigration counsel.
